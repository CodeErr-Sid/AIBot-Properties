Featured Properties

  • Peace Lagoons

    Peace Lagoons is an innovative residential project located in Dubai Land Residence Complex, offering studio, 1, and 2-bedroom apartments. This luxurious complex features unique amenities such as a lagoon within the apartment area, private pools for each residence, a kids' play area, volleyball court, gymnasium, yoga area, and a 30,000 sqft shopping mall. Peace Lagoons combines elegance and modern living, redefining luxury with a 1% monthly payment plan.

  • Venera at The Valley Phase 2

    Venera at The Valley Phase 2 offers elegantly designed 3 and 4-bedroom townhouses. Located in Dubai, this development is inspired by nature, featuring rolling hills, lush meadows, and scenic landscapes. Residents can enjoy amenities like sports courts, kids' play areas, community centers, and adventure trails. The townhouses are part of a vibrant community that offers a luxury living experience intertwined with nature, providing a serene yet adventurous lifestyle.

  • 360 Riverside Crescent

    360 Riverside Crescent is a prestigious 57-story residential development by Sobha Realty located in Sobha Hartland 2, Meydan, Dubai. It features luxury 1, 1.5, and 2-bedroom apartment. The project is known for its diamond-shaped architectural design and offers a resort-style living experience. Amenities include a state-of-the-art infinity pool, jacuzzi, sports facilities like paddle tennis and squash courts, and sky gardens on the 18th and 43rd floors offering stunning views. The project is slated for handover in January 2027.
